42nd IEEE International Conference on Data Engineering (ICDE) Montréal, Canada - May 04-08, 2026

Welcome to ICDE 2026

The annual IEEE International Conference on Data Engineering (ICDE) is one of the premier conferences in data and information engineering and addresses research issues on designing, building, managing, and evaluating advanced data intensive systems and applications. It is a leading forum for researchers, practitioners, developers, and users to explore cutting-edge ideas and to exchange techniques, tools, and experiences.

Each year, ICDE attracts researchers and practitioners from the academia, industry and government from across the world. The audience includes leading data scientists and professors, entrepreneurs, developers, talented young researchers/students and other thought leaders from major vendors and academia.

ICDE 2026 will take place in Montreal from May 04-08, 2026. Nestled between the St. Lawrence and Rivière des Prairies, Montreal is a city where nature and history come together in harmony. As one of North America’s oldest cities, it offers a unique blend of French colonial heritage, cobblestone streets, and centuries-old architecture alongside modern innovation. Surrounded by waterways and green spaces, including the iconic Mount Royal Park at its heart, the city invites visitors to explore scenic riverfronts, lush trails, and panoramic views. With its rich cultural tapestry, historic charm, and deep connection to the natural landscape, Montreal offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.
